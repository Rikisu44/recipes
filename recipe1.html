Recipe for the egg noodles

Here are a few delicious egg noodle recipes, ranging from simple and comforting to hearty and flavorful:

ingridents

  • 8 oz egg noodles
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: parsley, grated Parmesan

steps

  1. Cook egg noodles in salted water according to package instructions. Drain.
  2. Toss hot noodles with butter, salt, and pepper.
  3. Optional: Sprinkle with chopped parsley or Parmesan.
