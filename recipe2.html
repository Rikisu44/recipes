Here are a few delicious pizza recipes, ranging from simple and comforting to hearty and flavorful:

ingridents

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

steps

  1. Make the Dough:
    • In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5–10 mins.
    • Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead until smooth (about 8–10 mins).
    • Cover and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  2. Prepare the Pizza:
    • Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
    • Roll out the dough into a 10–12 inch circle.
    • Place on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
    • Spread sauce, sprinkle cheese, and add basil.
  3. Olive oil (for drizzling)
    • Bake for 10–15 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese bubbly.
    • Drizzle with olive oil and serve hot.
